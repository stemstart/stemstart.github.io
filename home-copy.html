Specifically, we bring about quality STEM education to youth in our community by taking on the following responsibilities

Conducting market research on interesting and less mainstream STEM applications, and finding corresponding STEM education organizations

Partnering and coordinating with STEM organizations

Establishing the venue and date

Booking facilities in local libraries, schools and community centers

Designing flyers and other promotional materials 

Advertising the workshops--on social media, at community events for youth and parents, on school web platforms, and through the dissemination of promotional materials

Coordinating with school administration, teachers, parents and students from numerous local schools while promoting the workshop

Registering students before the workshops and handling ​registration on the day of workshop

Recruiting and training expert volunteers

Preparing registered participants with pre-reqs for workshops as relevant

Setting up and cleaning up the exhibits and learning materials on the day of the workshop. 

Providing onsite support during the day of the workshop
